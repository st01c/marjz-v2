For Vector Festival 2021, I convened a discussion panel featuring Sarah Friend (artist and blockchain developer, Berlin/GER), and Mitchell F Chan (artist, Toronto/CAN). (Ruth Catlow, co-founder Furtherfield, London/UK, was originally also meant to join us). The title of our very stimulating conversation was On De/centralization: Blockchain Art and New Forms of Community-Building, and a recording of it is also available on YouTube.
